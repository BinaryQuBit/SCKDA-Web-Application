Low Fidelity Prototype

A Little Activity

We will be doing a little activity today. We will give you a piece of paper that shows multiple headings of various pages. They are divided into three columns and these columns are our prototypes. We will explain the design respective to the paper you have and all you have to do is circle which prototype you like.

Rules while picking

  • You have to think like a visitor. Do not think like a member or a board member
  • You are not allowed to have a conversation between yourselves
  • You get only 5 seconds to mark the design you like
  • You are not allowed to ask question while picking the design

    • After 5 seconds; we will explain the next portion of the design and you are allowed to ask any question about anything or give us any sort of feedback, criticism, additions, takeouts etc.
